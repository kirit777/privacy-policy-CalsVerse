Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

DrinkEatMed ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you. The app may store locally:

• Reminder schedules (water, food, medicine) • Notification preferences • App usage settings • Streak and tracking data

All such data is stored locally on your device.

3. Permissions

DrinkEatMed may request the following permissions:

• Notification Permission – to send reminders • Exact Alarm Permission – for precise reminder timing

These permissions are used strictly for core app functionality.

4. Advertising

Our app may display ads using third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous data such as device information for personalized ads. We do not control how third-party advertisers use your data.

5. Data Storage

All reminder data and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

DrinkEatMed does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no digital storage method is 100% secure.
