Privacy Policy

FoodClear – Diet & Allergy Ingredient Scanner

1. Introduction

FoodClear ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We do NOT sell your personal data. We do NOT share your scans with third parties.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Camera access (to scan barcodes and ingredient labels)
  • Food ingredient scan results (processed locally on your device)
  • App usage analytics (if enabled)

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as health records or medical history.

3. Camera Usage

FoodClear requires camera access to:

  • Scan product barcodes or QR codes
  • Read ingredient labels using text recognition

Camera access is only active while scanning. We do NOT record, store, or upload camera footage.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze food ingredients for allergens
  • To show diet compatibility results
  • To improve app performance

5. Data Storage

Scan results and preferences are stored locally on your device unless cloud features are explicitly enabled in the future.

You may clear your scan history anytime from the Settings section.

6. Third-Party Services

FoodClear may use third-party services such as:

  • Google ML Kit (for text recognition)
  • Google Play Services
  • Analytics providers (if enabled)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

FoodClear does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Medical Disclaimer

FoodClear provides ingredient analysis for informational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice. Always verify information on the product label and consult a healthcare professional.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
